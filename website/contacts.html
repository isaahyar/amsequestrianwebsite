About

AMS Equestrian Club (AMSEC) was founded on the 15th of August, 2016. Our main goal is to provide an equestrian centre that has proper riding facilities for the safety and comfort of our riders and horses. AMSEC is also dedicated to providing the best means of equine training and education under the supervision of the Malaysian Equine Council and the Equestrian Association of Malaysia.
